Position Description

Seasonal Caretaker - Overlook Hotel

The Overlook Hotel is seeking a reliable and self-motivated individual to serve as Seasonal Caretaker during the winter months. The position begins shortly before the first snowfall and continues until the hotel reopens in spring. The Caretaker will be responsible for overseeing the property while it remains closed to guests. Duties include monitoring the building, performing routine inspections, maintaining essential equipment, and ensuring the hotel remains secure throughout the season. This unique opportunity offers a quiet working environment, breathtaking mountain scenery, and the rare chance to experience one of Colorado's most remarkable hotels in complete solitude. Applicants should be comfortable spending extended periods in an isolated location and capable of working independently. The ideal candidate may also find the peaceful atmosphere particularly suitable for personal projects such as writing, research, or other creative pursuits.

Requirements

  • Minimum age of 21 years.
  • Ability to remain on-site throughout the winter season (about 4-6 months).
  • Strong sense of responsibility and self-discipline.
  • Basic maintenance and troubleshooting skills.
  • Comfortable working in remote and isolated environments.
  • Good physical condition and ability to perform routine inspections.
  • No history of severe claustrophobia or discomfort with prolonged solitude.
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Previous experience in hospitality, maintenance, security, or property management is considered an advantage.
  • Willingness to follow all hotel regulations and procedures.
